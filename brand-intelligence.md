## Official identity
The current Vanta website publicly serves an official SVG wordmark and the press page links to a brand-assets package. The execution environment could identify the official asset URLs but could not retrieve the binary through its available network and Drive connectors. To avoid hotlinking, tracing, redrawing, or committing an unverified third-party copy, this campaign uses a restrained typeset `Vanta` identifier above the fold and records this exact technical limitation. No custom pseudo-logo replaces the company identity.

## Color and typography evidence
Vanta describes itself as a vibrant purple company and says its 2023 identity added supporting green, blue, and yellow colors. The public brand article names Atak and Nib as its new typefaces and explicitly describes a serif-led system balancing technology with compliance rigor. Proprietary font files are not copied. The campaign uses Georgia as a licensed/system serif substitute and Trebuchet/Avenir/Segoe UI as a humanist sans substitute, reproducing contrast, hierarchy, and editorial rhythm rather than claiming the substitutes are official.

## Visual language
Official public evidence describes:
- Ilma, a warm and intelligent llama mascot associated with vigilant protection;
- architectural illustration paired with soft expressive character;
- fur-inspired cutouts that hold photography, screenshots, or color;
- expressive large-scale iconography and functional monolinear iconography;
- purple as the anchor color, supported by green, blue, and yellow;
- accessible digital color use.

## Campaign translation
- Visible identity: typeset Vanta identifier plus `Candidate vision by Russell Dudek`.
- Signature visual: a dark-purple People Automation Warranty that assembles and settles into a verified state.
- Candidate-original accent: coral is reserved for human-authority and exception states; it is subordinate to the Vanta palette.
- Fur-inspired cutout geometry appears sparingly behind the hero artifact.
- The site uses editorial serif/sans contrast, architectural spacing, monolinear rules, and restrained motion.
- Documents carry the same purple, lilac, serif/sans hierarchy, rules, and warranty grammar without imitating official Vanta letterhead.

## Independent-candidate distinction
Every public surface identifies the work as an independent candidate vision and states that it is not affiliated with or endorsed by Vanta.
