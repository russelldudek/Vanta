Launch questions

Right problem?

Is manual effort the real constraint, or is the work unclear, unowned, or unnecessary?

Right mechanism?

Does AI add value beyond rules, scripts, integrations, or process redesign?

Right authority?

Could the workflow affect fairness, compensation, employment decisions, privacy, or candidate trust?

Right evidence?

Can the team observe value, errors, exceptions, adoption, and maintenance cost?
