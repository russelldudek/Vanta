Through DudeWorth, I develop AI-readiness and agentic workflow patterns that connect structured context, retrieval, human judgment, escalation, evaluation, governance, adoption burden, and time-to-value. Earlier roles at Amazon and Compunetics built the launch discipline, quality systems, root-cause practice, and cross-functional operating cadence required to keep fast-moving systems reliable.

I would bring that mechanism-building approach into Vanta’s Recruiting and People teams.
